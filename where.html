Where to Play

Now that you know how the pieces move, it's time to get playing. There are a number of online chess sites to play chess both against a computer and against other people over the internet. The following are 2 of my favorite sites to get started playing right away

Chess.com

Chess.com is a great place to play chess online. You can play against the computer and against other people. They also have tons of puzzles and tactics to increase your chess skills as you want to expand into the intermediate level.

lichess

lichess is another excellent site to play online against people and the computer. It is an open source website that offers a lot of the same great puzzles and tactics to build your skills as your gameplay improves.
